## DELIVERY BEST PRACTICES

1. **Pause before memorable phrases**
   - Let audience prepare to remember
   - Slow delivery of phrase
   - Pause after phrase lands

2. **Visual support**
   - Key phrases on slides (large text)
   - Nothing else competing for attention
   - Repeat visually at end (summary slide)

3. **Repetition strategy**
   - "Your intent is the layout" - say 2-3 times
   - Callback to "60 years" at closing
   - Bookend opening/closing with paradigm language

4. **Third-party quotes**
   - Always attribute source
   - Show source logo if possible
   - Emphasize credibility (IBM, Apple, Fraunhofer)
